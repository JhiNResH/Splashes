Adult Lap Swimming

Get Fit & Stay Fit With Daily Lap Swimming

Total Body Workout

Adult lap swimming provides a full-body workout that targets the core, arms, back, and legs. It also provides swimmers with access to various training methods including strength training, aerobic training, and low-impact training.

Important Guidelines:

  • Each lane will have up to two lap swimmers.
  • Children under the age of 14 must be accompanied by their parent and able to swim 200 yards.
  • No private swim instruction or coaching is permitted.
  • No Lifeguard on duty.

NOTE: If you would like to reserve the entire lane for yourself, and not have to share with another swimmer, you may reserve both A and B slots for your selected lane/time.
